Who We Are

III-VI Nanomaterials Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysia-based supplier specializing in III-VI group semiconductor nanomaterials, including sulfides, selenides, tellurides, and related compounds. We connect researchers and manufacturers with high-quality materials sourced from trusted global producers.

About Us

Your Specialist Partner for Semiconductor Nanomaterials

Established in Malaysia, we serve universities, semiconductor machinery manufacturers, aerospace, medical device companies, and thermal management industries. Our portfolio spans III-VI compounds, nitrides, carbides, graphene, nanopowders, and advanced ceramics.

We work closely with customers to understand their exact material requirements — purity, particle size, morphology, and packaging — ensuring every order meets the demands of your application.
