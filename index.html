Who We Are

A specialist supplier of advanced & nano-structured materials

III-VI Nanomaterials Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian company dedicated to sourcing and supplying high-performance materials — from advanced technical ceramics and semiconductor compounds to cutting-edge 2D materials and engineered nanopowders.

We work closely with manufacturers, research institutions and engineering teams to deliver materials with the purity, particle size and specifications their applications demand.
